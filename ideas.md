## Idea 1: "Obsidian Glass" — Dark Luxury Glassmorphism

**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalist Glassmorphism meets Dark Luxury

**Core Principles:**
- Deep obsidian backgrounds with layered glass panels at varying opacity levels
- Chromatic accent system using electric indigo (#6366F1) and cyan (#06B6D4) as dual highlights
- Typography as architecture — large display numerals and section labels as structural elements
- Every surface has depth: triple-layer shadows, inner glow, and frosted glass blur

**Color Philosophy:**
- Base: #0A0A0F (near-black with blue undertone), #0F0F1A (card backgrounds)
- Glass: rgba(255,255,255,0.04) to rgba(255,255,255,0.08) with backdrop-blur-xl
- Accents: Indigo #6366F1 (primary), Cyan #06B6D4 (secondary), Rose #F43F5E (destructive)
- Text: #F8FAFC (primary
